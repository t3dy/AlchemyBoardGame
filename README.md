A single-page browser game prototype built with pure HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript.

## Setup
Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser to play.

## Gameplay
- **Status Bar**: Monitor your Gold, Salts, Elixir, Productivity, Stability, and Health.
- **Lab Grid**: Purchase furniture to increase your base productivity.
- **Actions**: Choose to "Play It Safe" or "Push Myself" to draw cards and progress the round.
- **Goal**: Achieve a Productivity score of 12 (Magnum Opus).
- **Failure**: Don't let your Stability collapse twice or your health hit Incapacitated (ðŸ”´) twice.

## Tech Stack
- HTML5
- CSS3 (Parchment aesthetic)
- Vanilla JavaScript
